About Us

At Mzalas Kota, we believe a kota is more than just a meal – it’s a taste of home, friendship, and good vibes. We started with one simple idea: to serve fresh, affordable, and mouth-watering kotas that bring people together. From school kids grabbing a quick lunch to families sharing a hearty bite, our kotas are made for everyone.
What makes us different?\
  • Fresh Ingredients – We prepare everything daily, from golden fries to sizzling Russians and juicy patties.
  • Local Flavor – We keep it authentic with atchar, cheese, and all the classics you grew up loving.
  • Community First – We’re proud to be part of the neighborhood, creating a space where everyone feels welcome.
